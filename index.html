Welcome

Memory Lane

Mortal Kombat propelled itself into the spotlight due to the controversial nature of the game. Specifically, the game allowed you to perform a "Fatality," which required the user to Entire a secret button combination to perform violent finishing moves on his/her dazed opponent. Kids would read through their favorite gaming magazines looking secret moves. Whoever found them first would bring their hand-written notes and let other kids in class copy them.

Back then, video games were released in arcades months before they were released on home console. Sometimes you'd be lucky enough to find an empty arcade cabinet, but more often than not there would already be a few players at the machine. Some of these players played like experts. They knew every move, secret stage finishers, how to fight hidden characters, and how to easily take down the end bosses in the game. We would hurry and read the character's ending as fast as we could so we could tell our friends what happens for each character.

Mortal Kombat was more than just a violent video game. It was a game full of hidden secrets and easter eggs. It had interesting characters with back stories as if it was its own comic book universe.

The Strategy Guide

Gaming strategy guides were popular items before the internet became a common household service. These guides touched on everything that made the game interesting. They contained all of the special moves, finshing moves, biographies, and character endings. They went through the meaning of all of the hidden messages within the game and even discussed rumors. Friends and I would buy these guides and read through them looking for anything that we may have missed.

About This Application

When I was a kid, I would often start designing my own strategy guides using colored pens. I loved the idea of digging into the game myself and trying to find my own secret combinations. I would read through the end credits hoping to find a subtle hint that others may have missed. I never did finish a guide, but better late than never.

I am currently studying Front End Web Development and I couldn't think of a better project to marry my learning efforts with my interests. I've had a blast designing this application. I hope that this offers a sense of nostalgia to those who find it.

Move Key

  • U: UP
  • D: DOWN
  • F: FORWARD
  • B: BACK
  • HP: HIGH PUNCH
  • LP: LOW PUNCH
  • LK: LOW KICK
  • HK: HIGH KICK
  • BL: BLOCK
  • +: PRESS BUTTONS TOGETHER

Basic Moves

  • FORWARD: Move Forward
  • BACKWARD: Move Backward
  • UP: Jump
  • DOWN: Duck
  • UP + FORWARD: Forward Front Tuck
  • UP + BACKWARD: Backward Back Tuck
  • HIGH PUNCH
  • LOW PUNCH
  • HIGH KICK
  • LOW KICK
  • DOWN + HIGH PUNCH: Uppercut
  • DOWN + LOW PUNCH: Ducking Punch
  • HIGH PUNCH (Close): Backfist
  • LOW PUNCH (Close): Throw Opponent
  • BACK + HIGH KICK: Spinning Kick
  • BACK + LOW KICK: Sweep
  • UP + DIRECTION + KICK: Flying Kick
  • UP + DIRECTION + PUNCH: Flying Punch
  • BLOCK: High Block
  • DOWN + BLOCK: Low Block

The Pit Fatality

At The Pit Stage, perform a Uppercut when instructed to finish your opponent to knock them off of the bridge.

For ages, it was a competition of honor and glory. Noble warriors from all around the globe were invited tot ake part, each seeking to win the title of Grand Champion.

But that was a long time ago... before the tournament was corrupted by the evil demon Shang Tsung; A warrior who took not only the lives of his opponents, but their very souls.

With the aid of his pupil, Goro (A hideous half-human dragon), Tsung began a domination of the contest which has lasted for centuries. Today, 500 years later, the contest begins anew as warriors once again gather to partake in Mortal Kombat.

Goro Lives

A 2,000 year old, half-human dragon, Goro remains undefeated for the past 500 years. He won the title of Grand Champion by defeating Kung Lao, A Shaolin Fighting monk. It was during this period that the tournament fell into Shang Tsung's hands and was corrupted.

Finding Reptile

Reptile can be found at The Pit stage. After meeting the conditions listed below, players will fight Reptile at the bottom of The Pit.

  • You must be fighting at The Pit stage
  • A sihlouette must glide past the moon
  • You must achieve a Double Flawless Victory
  • You must not use block
  • You must perform a Fatality(Pit Stage Fatality does not count)
